Solar Radiation Enters the Earth's Atmosphere...

... and some reflects off Earth's surface.

It should escape Earth's atmosphere...

...but with humans burning fossil fuels, greenhouse gases are emitted into the atmosphere...

...which insulate the Earth and cause the radiation to be trapped in the Earth's atmosphere instead of escaping into space.

The aim is to try and let as many sun rays escape as possible by rotating the Earth and letting the radiation out where there isn't any greenhouse gases. They will bounce off the Earth and the greenhouse gases. Ensure there are less than 10 rays in Earth's atmosphere at any given point. Otherwise, Earth has become too hot and you lose.
