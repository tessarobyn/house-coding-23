Solar radiation enters the Earth's atmosphere...

... and some of the radiation reflects off Earth's surface.

It should escape Earth's atmosphere...

...but with humans burning fossil fuels, lots of greenhouse gases are emitted into the atmosphere...

...which insulate the Earth and cause the radiation to be trapped in the Earth's atmosphere instead of escaping into space. This is causes Earth's temperature to increase.

Aim

The aim is to try and let as much solar radiation escape as possible by rotating the Earth and letting the radiation out where there aren't any greenhouse gases. The radiation will bounce off the Earth and the greenhouse gases, hence becoming trapped in Earth's atmosphere.

Ensure there are less than 10 rays in Earth's atmosphere at any given point. Otherwise, Earth has become too hot and you lose.
